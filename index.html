LifterCalc

Created by Perlin Lim, LifterCalc is a one-stop shop for powerlifters to calculate their powerlifting scores and points. Users can immediately see and compare their scores across not just the currently used systems of IPF GL, DOTS, and Wilks-2, but also older formulas like Wilks and IPF.
Github
Formulas are pulled from this pdf by the International Powerlifting Federation
